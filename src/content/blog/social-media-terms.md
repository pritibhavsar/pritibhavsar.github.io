## A ##

- [Affiliate marketing](/blog/social-media-terms/affiliate-marketing) Performance model where partners earn commission from tracked conversions.
- [Algospeak](/blog/social-media-terms/algospeak) Alternative wording used to avoid moderation triggers.
- [Algorithm](/blog/social-media-terms/social-algorithm) Platform rules that rank and distribute content.
- [Analytics](/blog/social-media-terms/analytics) Data and analysis used to evaluate social performance.

## B ##

- [Batching](/blog/social-media-terms/batching) Grouping similar work into focused production blocks.
- [BeReal](/blog/social-media-terms/bereal) Social app centered around one unfiltered daily post.
- [Bio](/blog/social-media-terms/bio) Short profile text that explains identity, value, and links.
- [Bluesky](/blog/social-media-terms/bluesky) Decentralized social platform and X/Twitter alternative.
- [Boomerang](/blog/social-media-terms/boomerang) Looping short video format popularized by Instagram.
- [Boosted post](/blog/social-media-terms/boosted-post) Organic post promoted with paid budget.
- [Brand awareness](/blog/social-media-terms/brand-awareness) How recognizable and memorable a brand is.

## C ##

- [CAC](/blog/social-media-terms/cac) Customer acquisition cost across marketing and sales activities.
- [Campaign](/blog/social-media-terms/campaign) Coordinated set of posts tied to one objective.
- [Caption](/blog/social-media-terms/caption) Text that accompanies social media visuals.
- [Carousel post](/blog/social-media-terms/carousel-post) Multi-slide post with sequential images or videos.
- [CFBR](/blog/social-media-terms/cfbr) "Comment for better reach" engagement tactic.
- [Character counter](/blog/social-media-terms/character-counter) Tool to track text length per platform limits.
- [Clickbait](/blog/social-media-terms/clickbait) Overpromising content intended to maximize clicks.
- [Community manager](/blog/social-media-terms/community-manager) Person managing audience interaction and trust.
- [Content pillars](/blog/social-media-terms/content-pillars) Core topic themes guiding content strategy.
- [Creator economy](/blog/social-media-terms/creator-economy) Ecosystem where creators monetize audience and content.
- [Cross-posting](/blog/social-media-terms/cross-posting) Publishing the same content across multiple channels.
- [Crowdsourcing](/blog/social-media-terms/crowdsourcing) Collecting ideas or contributions from communities.

## D ##

- [Decentralized](/blog/social-media-terms/decentralized) Platform model distributed across independent servers.
- [Demure](/blog/social-media-terms/demure) Trend term associated with modest or understated presentation.
- [DM](/blog/social-media-terms/dm) Direct message sent privately between accounts.
- [Duet (TikTok)](/blog/social-media-terms/tiktok-duet) Split-screen reaction or collaboration format.

## E ##

- [Emoji](/blog/social-media-terms/emoji) Visual symbols used to add tone and meaning.
- [Engagement rate](/blog/social-media-terms/engagement-rate) Ratio of interactions relative to audience size or reach.
- [Evergreen content](/blog/social-media-terms/evergreen-content) Content that stays relevant for long periods.

## F ##

- [Fediverse](/blog/social-media-terms/fediverse) Interconnected network of decentralized social platforms.
- [Feed](/blog/social-media-terms/feed) Stream of content shown in a social app.
- [Finsta](/blog/social-media-terms/finsta) Secondary private Instagram account with casual content.
- [Follower growth](/blog/social-media-terms/follower-growth) Increase in audience size over time.
- [FYP](/blog/social-media-terms/fyp) TikTok "For You Page" recommendation feed.

## G ##

- [Generation Z (Gen Z)](/blog/social-media-terms/gen-z) Demographic cohort born roughly 1997 to 2012.
- [GIF](/blog/social-media-terms/gif) Short looping animated image format.
- [Google Business Profile](/blog/social-media-terms/google-business-profile) Business listing system for local discovery.
- [GRWM](/blog/social-media-terms/grwm) "Get Ready With Me" creator content format.

## H ##

- [Hashtag](/blog/social-media-terms/hashtag) Metadata label using `#` to categorize discoverable content.

## I ##

- [Impressions](/blog/social-media-terms/impressions) Total number of times content appears on screen.
- [Influencer](/blog/social-media-terms/influencer) Creator whose audience trust can shape decisions.
- [Instagram Broadcast Channel](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-broadcast-channel) One-to-many creator messaging channel.
- [Instagram Close Friends](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-close-friends) Private story sharing list.
- [Instagram Highlights](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-highlights) Saved story collections pinned to profile.
- [Instagram Insights](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-insights) Native analytics for business or creator accounts.
- [Instagram Notes](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-notes) Short status-like messages inside Instagram inbox.
- [Instagram Reels](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-reels) Short-form vertical video format.
- [Instagrammable](/blog/social-media-terms/instagrammable) Visually appealing enough to be shared online.

## J ##

- [JPEG](/blog/social-media-terms/jpeg) Compressed image format widely used on the web.

## K ##

- [Keyword](/blog/social-media-terms/keyword) Search term used to find content or products.
- [KPI](/blog/social-media-terms/kpi) Key performance indicator tied to measurable goals.
- [KOL](/blog/social-media-terms/kol) Key opinion leader with influence in a niche.

## L ##

- [Link in bio](/blog/social-media-terms/link-in-bio) Primary outbound profile link for social traffic routing.
- [LinkedIn Articles](/blog/social-media-terms/linkedin-articles) Long-form posts published directly on LinkedIn.
- [LinkedIn Company Page](/blog/social-media-terms/linkedin-company-page) Brand profile used for visibility and hiring.
- [LinkedIn Creator Mode](/blog/social-media-terms/linkedin-creator-mode) Profile mode built for creator discovery.
- [LinkedIn Newsletter](/blog/social-media-terms/linkedin-newsletter) Recurring publication sent to subscribers.
- [LinkedIn Showcase Page](/blog/social-media-terms/linkedin-showcase-page) Sub-page focused on product or segment stories.
- [Long-form video](/blog/social-media-terms/long-form-video) Extended-duration video designed for depth.

## M ##

- [Main character energy](/blog/social-media-terms/main-character-energy) Trend phrase for personal narrative confidence.
- [Marketing funnel](/blog/social-media-terms/marketing-funnel) Journey from awareness to conversion and retention.
- [Meme](/blog/social-media-terms/meme) Culturally shared, remixable humor format.
- [Meta Business Suite](/blog/social-media-terms/meta-business-suite) Management hub for Facebook and Instagram activity.
- [Meta Verified](/blog/social-media-terms/meta-verified) Subscription verification and account support bundle.

## N ##

- [NPS](/blog/social-media-terms/nps) Net promoter score measuring customer advocacy.

## O ##

- [OOTD](/blog/social-media-terms/ootd) "Outfit of the day" post format.
- [Organic marketing](/blog/social-media-terms/organic-marketing) Unpaid methods for audience and demand growth.

## P ##

- [PFP](/blog/social-media-terms/pfp) Profile picture representing an account identity.
- [Photo dump](/blog/social-media-terms/photo-dump) Casual multi-image post with mixed moments.
- [POV](/blog/social-media-terms/pov) "Point of view" storytelling format in short video.

## Q ##

- [Quiet mode](/blog/social-media-terms/quiet-mode) Notification pause mode in social applications.
- [Quality control](/blog/social-media-terms/quality-control) Review process for consistency, clarity, and brand safety.

## R ##

- [Reach](/blog/social-media-terms/reach) Unique users who saw a piece of content.
- [Reddit](/blog/social-media-terms/reddit) Community-driven forum platform organized by subreddits.
- [ROI](/blog/social-media-terms/roi) Return on investment from social initiatives.

## S ##

- [Sentiment analysis](/blog/social-media-terms/sentiment-analysis) Detecting emotional tone in public mentions.
- [Shadow banned](/blog/social-media-terms/shadow-banned) Reduced visibility without explicit user-facing notice.
- [Short-form video](/blog/social-media-terms/short-form-video) Brief video optimized for fast attention and completion.
- [Smart goals](/blog/social-media-terms/smart-goals) Goals defined as specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound.
- [Social media](/blog/social-media-terms/social-media) Digital platforms for publishing, interaction, and community.
- [Sponsored post](/blog/social-media-terms/sponsored-post) Post promoted with paid distribution.
- [Stickers](/blog/social-media-terms/stickers) Interactive overlays in stories and short videos.
- [Stitch (TikTok)](/blog/social-media-terms/tiktok-stitch) Add your clip after a selected segment of another video.

## T ##

- [Threads (Instagram)](/blog/social-media-terms/instagram-threads) Text-first social platform by Instagram.
- [Thought leader](/blog/social-media-terms/thought-leader) Recognized expert known for clear original perspective.
- [TikTok challenge](/blog/social-media-terms/tiktok-challenge) Prompt-based trend inviting user participation.
- [TikTok Shop](/blog/social-media-terms/tiktok-shop) In-app commerce feature for discovery and checkout.
- [Touch grass](/blog/social-media-terms/touch-grass) Slang reminder to disconnect and go offline.
- [Trending](/blog/social-media-terms/trending) Topic or format experiencing rapid visibility growth.
- [Twitter threads](/blog/social-media-terms/twitter-threads) Connected post sequence forming one longer narrative.

## U ##

- [UGC](/blog/social-media-terms/ugc) User-generated content created by customers or communities.
- [URL shortener](/blog/social-media-terms/url-shortener) Tool for compact, trackable links.
- [UX](/blog/social-media-terms/ux) Overall quality of user interaction with a digital product.

## V ##

- [Vanish mode](/blog/social-media-terms/vanish-mode) Auto-disappearing chat mode in messaging apps.
- [Viral](/blog/social-media-terms/viral) Rapid spread of content across audiences.
- [Vlog](/blog/social-media-terms/vlog) Video-based blog format.

## W ##

- [Watermark](/blog/social-media-terms/watermark) Overlay marking ownership or source attribution.

## X ##

- [X/Twitter Advanced Search](/blog/social-media-terms/twitter-advanced-search) Filtered search tool for targeted discovery.
- [X App](/blog/social-media-terms/x-app) Rebranded Twitter platform identity.

## Y ##

- [YouTube Studio](/blog/social-media-terms/youtube-studio) Creator dashboard for channel management and analytics.

## Z ##

- [Zapier](/blog/social-media-terms/zapier) Automation platform that connects apps and workflows.
